About Pakistan

Pakistan is a South Asian country located between India, Afghanistan, Iran, and China. It has a diverse landscape ranging from mountains in the north to deserts in the south. The country is home to the Indus River, which supports agriculture and civilization. Pakistan has a rich history influenced by ancient cultures and Islamic heritage. Islamabad is the capital, while Karachi is the largest city and economic hub.

Islamabad is the capital city, while Karachi is the largest city and main economic hub. Pakistan’s economy is based on agriculture, textiles, and industry. The country has a growing population and a strong cultural identity with traditions, languages, and festivals. Despite facing economic and political challenges, Pakistan continues to develop in various sectors.
