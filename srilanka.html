About srilanka

Sri Lanka is an island nation located in the Indian Ocean, just south of India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, ancient cities, and beautiful landscapes. The country has a tropical climate with lush forests, beaches, and wildlife. Sri Lanka has a long history influenced by Buddhism, which plays a major role in its culture and traditions. The island is also famous for tea production, especially Ceylon tea, which is exported worldwide.

The country is divided into different regions including coastal plains, central highlands, and forest areas. Colombo is the largest city and economic center, while Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte is the official capital. Sri Lanka has a developing economy based on agriculture, tourism, and industry. Despite facing challenges in the past, the country continues to grow and attract visitors with its natural beauty and cultural diversity.
