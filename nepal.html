About Nepal

Nepal is a landlocked South Asian country known for its majestic Himalayas, rich culture, and diverse geography. It lies between India and China and covers an area of about 147,516 square kilometers with a population of around 29 million people.

The country is divided into three regions: the mountains, hills, and Terai. Home to Mount Everest, Nepal is famous for trekking and mountaineering. Kathmandu, the capital, is also the largest cultural and economic center.
