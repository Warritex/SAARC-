About maldives

Maldives is a small island nation located in the Indian Ocean, southwest of Sri Lanka and India. It consists of thousands of coral islands grouped into atolls. The country is famous for its clear blue waters, white sandy beaches, and marine life. Tourism is the main source of income, attracting visitors from around the world.

Malé is the capital and main city of the Maldives. The country has a small population and limited land area. Due to its low elevation, Maldives is highly vulnerable to climate change and rising sea levels. The government is working on environmental protection and sustainable tourism. Despite its size, Maldives is well known globally for its natural beauty and luxury resorts.
